Domain interpretation from the human review:

- `normative_form`: manufacturer order/product forms for pump or heat-exchanger
  equipment; structure and manufacturer content match while selected equipment
  and customer values differ;
- `estimate_boilerplate`: standard local-estimate pages and tables generated by
  GRAND-Smeta or equivalent estimate templates;
- `shared_technical_content`: pump performance diagrams in a shared
  manufacturer frame, with curves differing by equipment model and parameters.

Main result:

- all 30 were high-overlap, cross-organization, rare-text near-matches;
- none was judged a borrowing candidate;
- therefore structural similarity + rare shared text + strong text Jaccard is
  insufficient for a copy/borrowing verdict;
- the current batch is a valuable negative-control set for UC3;
- provenance/source classification must precede borrowing interpretation.

Frozen guidance:

1. Identify manufacturer/regulatory/industry forms.
2. Identify estimate/software-generated output.
3. Identify shared product or technical artifacts.
4. Consider project-specific direct reuse only after those explanations are
   excluded.
5. Use `uncertain` rather than forcing a borrowing label.
